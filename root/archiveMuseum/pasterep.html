Paste-Rep

Description

Habits dominate the fabric of our daily lives. In our efforts to be productive, we settle into patterns of comfort and pay little attention to the mundane constructs of those very habits. To demonstrate this notion of unintentional forgetfulness, the artists have decided to challenge the repetitive aspects of habit. Instead, they have decided to play with documentation by letting play into the mix. In this way the mundane no longer is mundane, but visually significant.
