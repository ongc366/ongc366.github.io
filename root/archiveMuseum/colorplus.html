Color++

Description

Rarely do we not digest color. However, what we do not digest about color is the sheer fact what we are actually seeing in the world is made out of color. Instead, we see the objects that the color encompasses first. In this series, the artists have brought color to the forefront and have decontextualized its nature from its container—the screen.
