Symbolist

Description

Dont Walk. Hazard. Restroom. These are all messages that one encounters in the form of signs or icons. But what if the conceptual intent of each icon is taken out of context? Does it still maintain its meaning, does it take on a new meaning, or does it take on a mixture of both? In this exhibition, the artists have taken a D.I.Y. approach to known symbols and have fabricated their own contexts for those very same symbols to live in. With this in mind, viewers then can judge for themselves what these symbols begin to mean for them in these new contexts.
